Get Involved

PorchFest is so successful every year because of its generous volunteers and sponsors.There are a number of ways you can participate in PorchFest!

  • Band or Musician

    Grab your guitar and showcase your musical talents for a few hours!

  • Porch Sponsor

    Give up your porch for the day! Must be a D.C. resident or homeowner.

  • Volunteer

    Run an information booth, direct visitors and hand out flyers!
