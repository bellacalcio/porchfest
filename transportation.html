Getting There

Once at the event, people usually walk, ride bikes or scooters, drive golf carts, or push strollers as they move from one porch to another along a determined route to partake in the festivities. However, we want to make sure you get to your desired location in a quick and easy manner.

Rhode Island Ave

  • Metro

    The easiest way to get to Rhode Island Ave is on the red line, just get off on the Rhode Island Ave-Brentwood stop

  • Car

    Parking on Rhode Island Ave Main Street will be limited. For those willing to brave street parking, the central address is 2300 Rhode Island Ave NE

  • Zipcar

    The Zipcar shuttle stops available at each porch - look for the signs! Hop on, hop off, free of charge!

Adams Morgan

  • Bus

    Adams Morgan is serviced by the 96 bus that goes from Capitol Hill area to Tenleytown-AU

  • Metro

    Adams Morgan is a 15-minute walk from the Woodley Park-Adams Morgan metro stop on the red line

  • Car

    On street parking is limited and there is no guaranteed parking for this event, but the central address for the event is Adams Mill Rd NW & Columbia Rd
